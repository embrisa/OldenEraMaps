Map objects: know your enemy before the fight

There’s no “load save” button in multiplayer. Every detail decides the outcome.
 Keeping in your head which objects give what reward, what guard is there, how they’re positioned,
 and which side to approach from — it’s simply impossible. We’re only human.

TheBattle Simulatorsolves this problem once and for all.
 Choose difficulty, object size, hit “Load” — and here’s the full picture:

Exact enemy placement, composition and stats — everything at your fingertips.
 Left-click a unit to see its full stats.

Calculate damage before the battle

Attacking an object? Don’t go in blind. In ranked one mistake = game over.
 Most strong strategies rely on your damage stack one-shotting the enemy.
 If even 5–10% remain — that’s a disaster that costs you the win.

In-game accurate damage calculation means formulas, attack/defense modifiers,
 skills like [Expert Offense], [Expert Defense],
 artifacts and abilities. All that in your head, under timer pressure? Forget it.

In theBattle Simulatorjust drag units ( [Lich Dragon], [Crossbowman]) onto the field, set the count. Upgraded version — left-click the icon. Quantity — click the number. Add your hero stats (attack) and enemy hero if needed (defence) — attackers on the left, defenders on the right.

Drag the attacker icon onto the target — and boom! Top-right corner shows exact damage to the stack.
 Before the fight even starts.

An advantage that’s borderline cheating

One of the core things in “Heroes” is full battle determinism. Same conditions = same outcome, always.
 Found the perfect way to clear an object with two damage stacks [Austringer]and [Swordsman]? It will work 100% of the time.

Before you had to memorize and grind hundreds of games. Now it’s easier.
 In theReplayssection of Battle Simulator pick your faction, spells,
 damage stacks and filter by objects. Get every possible winning variation — step by step,
 including enemy moves.

A legal “cheat”: even if you don’t know how to take a hard object,
 the simulator will write you a perfect plan for your army. Step by step. Ideal for ranked.
